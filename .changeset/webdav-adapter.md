Add a WebDAV adapter (`files-sdk/webdav`) backed by the `webdav` client. HTTP-based, with native server-side `copy`/`move`, ranged and streaming downloads, and content-type round-tripping. Works against Nextcloud, ownCloud, Apache `mod_dav`, `sabre/dav`, and other WebDAV servers. Available in the CLI and MCP as the `webdav` provider.
